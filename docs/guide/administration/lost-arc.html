# Lost ARC- What To Do

You lost your ARC? Well, there is a simple process to follow in case you loose your ARC.

  1. Go to the nearest neighborhood police station, report it and fill a form

  2. Prepare:

  • Passport
  • Passport size photo
  • Application form
  • Fees (10,000~30,000 KRW)
  1. Go to your allocated immigration office (by geographic location of you home address) within 2 weeks

NOTE

If you haven't got it within the period of 2 weeks, you might be subjected to some disadvantages when it comes to renewing your visa status.
